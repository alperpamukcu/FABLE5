# LAST CALL — GDD Module: Patrons (Jokers)

## 8. PATRONS (JOKERS) — THE HEART OF BUILD VARIETY

Patrons sit on a shelf-row at the top of the screen. **5 slots** by default. They are characters with names, one-line personalities, and passive effects. **60 at launch.** Rarity: 40 Common/Uncommon, 15 Rare, 5 Legendary. Sample table:

| Patron | Rarity | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Sailor Musa** | Common | +4 Mult if the Mix contains a Spirit. |
| **The Poet** | Common | +30 Flavor if the Mix has exactly 3 ingredients. |
| **Aunt Perihan** | Common | Sweet cards give +15 extra Flavor. |
| **The Night Cabbie** | Common | +$2 at end of each customer. |
| **Twins at Table 4** | Uncommon | Retrigger the first scored card. |
| **The Chemist** | Uncommon | Sour cards each give +2 Mult. |
| **Off-Duty Cop** | Uncommon | ×2 Mult if no Restocks were used this order. |
| **The Insomniac** | Uncommon | First Mix each customer: ×2 Mult. |
| **Jazz Singer** | Rare | ×3 Mult if the Mix is a Martini or Negroni. |
| **The Collector** | Rare | Gains +1 Mult permanently every time you mix a Perfect Serve (this run). |
| **Mysterious Stranger** | Rare | Sells for $20 more each Night he stays. |
| **The Owner's Cat** | Legendary | Copies the effect of the Patron to its right. |
| **Madame Zaza** | Legendary | Playing your Most-Mixed recipe: ×4 Mult. |

Patrons can be sold (half price, rounded up), reordered by drag, and can carry Quality tiers (a Signature Patron gives ×1.5 Mult on top of its effect). Design rule: every Ingredient Type, every Recipe, restock-count, money-count, and deck-size must each have at least 3 Patrons that care about it, so at least 8 distinct archetypes exist (Spirit-flood, Sour-mult, Restock-econ, Perfect-Serve, mono-Type "flush" builds, big-deck, thin-deck, money-scaling).
